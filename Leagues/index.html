What is Laser Leagues?

The Gamemode

3 Teams enter the arena and begin the game at their home base.

The goal of the game is to outscore the opposing teams by zapping them, destroying their bases, and defending your own base

A base will score you 3001 points, but shooting it will take points away. So make sure you are fully protected and have time to destroy it completely by zapping it 3 times.

Each game will last 12 minutes

Check our facebook page for a full breakdown of the rules and gamemode settings

How It Works

All registered teams will be placed into a round-robin draw spanning over the competition weeks

At the end of each game, "Comeptition Points" are awarded to the teams based on their placement

1st Place - 6 Competition Points

2nd Place - 4 Competition Points

3rd Place - 2 Competition Points

The team with the highest comeptition points at the end of the league will be deemed the winners

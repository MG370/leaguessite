Important Party Information

PLAYERS

A party booking must be a minimum of 8 players.

AGE

Underworld Laser is a game recommended for anyone 6 years or over. Guests 6 years old can play if accompanied by an adult and deemed confident enough to play by Underworld Laser staff.

FOOTWEAR

No thongs, no sandals, enclosed footwear only.
Underworld Laser will refuse entry if inappropriate footwear

PARENTS

At least one parent is to remain at Underworld Laser for the duration of the party. Due to the limited space at Underworld, there may not be room for more than 2 parents and the party guests to remain on the premises. Parents are welcome to enter Lollipops with their younger guests which is located right next to Underworld laser or feel free to make purchases at the Café.

DEPOSIT

A non-refundable deposit of $100 is to be paid at the time of booking. Your time only be guaranteed when a deposit has been paid.

ARENA

There is a maximum of 30 players within the Arena at any one time. Be careful with your numbers. We may have other parties booked at similar times, so be careful to stay as close to the number you originally booked with us.

GUEST LIST

We need final numbers confirmed. What you confirm is what you pay for. Please ensure you email your guest list no later than the Monday prior to the party.

FOOD

Your food & drink selection must be confirmed to our Party Co-Ordinator no later than the Monday prior to the party.

ARRIVAL TIME

Please invite your guest’s 10 minutes prior to the start of your scheduled party time. All missions must start on time and we are unable to wait for guests that are late or refund any missed missions.

PAYMENT

A full single payment must be made before the party starts.

SHARE THE FUN

Underworld Laser foyer & Party Areas are not exclusive during parties.

PARTY AREA

Use of the Party Area is reserved for 40 minutes only for Food & Cake and is not exclusive use.

GAME DURATION

Missions last for 10 minutes. A new Laser Mission starts every 12 minutes.

UPON ARRIVAL

All invoices must be settled prior to the party. Once you’ve registered, make yourself comfortable within Underworld Laser & we will make sure your guests are greeted & know what to do.

VALUABLES

Your guests will be moving around often for the duration of the party. However Underworld Laser allows you to place items in plastic tubs behind reception but Underworld Laser accepts no responsibility for your items.
